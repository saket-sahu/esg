WASHINGTON D.C. – July 1, 2022 – (Motor Sports NewsWire) –



 _An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash._



 **NHTSA Campaign Number:** 22V449000



 **Manufacturer:** Honda (American Honda Motor Co.)



 **Components:** ENGINE AND ENGINE COOLING



 **Potential Number of Units Affected:** 1,740



 **Summary:** Honda (American Honda Motor Co) is recalling certain 2020-2022
GL1800 Gold Wing motorcycles equipped with manual transmissions. An ignition
timing software error for the engine control unit (ECU) may reduce engine
performance, which can result in an engine stall.



 **Remedy:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Owner
notification letters are expected to be mailed August 20, 2022. Owners may
contact American Honda Motor Co customer service at 1-866-784-1870.



 **Notes:** Owners may also contact the National Highway Traffic Safety
Administration Vehicle Safety Hotline at 1-888-327-4236 (TTY 1-800-424-9153),
or go to www.nhtsa.gov.



 _Source: National Highway Traffic Safety Administration_
